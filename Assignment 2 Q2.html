Do cats know their name?

Cats are known for their independent spirit, but they do recognize their names. Studies show that while they can hear you calling, the response may vary depending on their mood!

Essential tips to raise healthy pets

Raising a happy pet involves regular vet check-ups, a balanced diet, daily playtime, and lots of love. Remember, a structured routine helps keep your feline friend content.

Why does my cat attack me?

Those sudden attacks on your ankles are not personal; it's just your cat's natural hunting instinct kicking in. Engage in regular play with toys to help them release this energy.

How to throw a birthday party for your cat

A cat birthday party should be a calm and comfortable affair. Consider some new toys and a special treat meal. And don't forget a photo session to capture the memories!
